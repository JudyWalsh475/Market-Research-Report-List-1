<p><strong>The Frozen Bread Dough Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fermented</li><li>Unfermented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frozen bread dough market is divided into two main types: fermented and unfermented. Fermented dough undergoes a yeast-leavening process before freezing, enhancing flavor and aroma, and leading to a more complex texture when baked. In contrast, unfermented dough is frozen before the fermentation process, offering a more straightforward baking experience, with less flavor complexity. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with fermented options appealing to those seeking artisanal qualities and unfermented options appealing to convenience-focused users.</p></p>
