<p><p>Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are minimally invasive medical tools used to treat brain aneurysms, which are abnormal bulges in blood vessels that can lead to life-threatening hemorrhages. These devices, typically made of soft, flexible materials, are inserted into the aneurysm via catheterization and deployed to occlude the aneurysm, preventing blood flow and reducing the risk of rupture. The market for these devices is experiencing significant growth due to rising incidences of vascular diseases, increased awareness of advanced treatment options, and technological innovations in device design and manufacturing. </p><p>The Aneurysm Coiling & Embolization Devices Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period. <p><p>The aneurysm coiling and embolization devices market comprises several key types aimed at treating vascular conditions. Embolic coils are used to occlude aneurysms by inducing clot formation, providing a minimally invasive solution. Flow diversion devices redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm, promoting healing. Liquid embolic agents consist of biocompatible substances that can be injected to fill and seal off blood vessels. Together, these advanced treatments enhance patient outcomes in managing cerebrovascular and other vascular diseases.</p></p>
