<p><strong>The Automatic Continuous Variable Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Connection</li><li>Chain Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Continuous Variable Transmission (CVT) market can be segmented into two primary types based on the connection mechanism: belt connection and chain connection. Belt connection CVTs utilize a flexible belt to transfer power between pulleys, offering a smoother driving experience and improved fuel efficiency. In contrast, chain connection CVTs use a metal chain, providing enhanced durability and strength for higher torque applications. Both types cater to different vehicle performance needs, influencing consumer preferences and automotive design trends.</p></p>
