<p><strong>The Fibre Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode Optical Cable</li><li>Multimode Optical Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic cables market is primarily divided into two types: single-mode optical cables and multimode optical cables. Single-mode cables have a narrow core diameter, allowing light to travel directly down the fiber, making them ideal for long-distance communication with high bandwidth. In contrast, multimode cables have a larger core, enabling multiple light paths, suitable for shorter distances and less expensive installations. Both types are crucial for telecommunications, data centers, and high-speed internet connections, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
