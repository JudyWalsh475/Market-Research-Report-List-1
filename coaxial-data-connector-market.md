<p><p>Coaxial data connectors are categorized into several types based on their locking mechanisms and assembly methods. Plug-in connectors allow for easy connection and disconnection, while push-pull connectors offer secure engagement with a simple push or pull action. Bayonet connectors feature a twisting mechanism for a secure fit, ensuring reliable connections in demanding environments. Crimp connectors rely on a mechanical compression technique for attachment, providing durability and stability. Other connectors encompass specialized designs that cater to unique applications and requirements.</p></p>
