<p><p>The high-purity aluminum nitride powder market primarily includes three production methods: the direct nitridation method, the carbothermal reduction and nitridation method, and the gas-phase synthesis method. The direct nitridation method involves heating aluminum powder in a nitrogen-rich environment, ensuring high purity. The carbothermal reduction method combines aluminum oxide with carbon in a nitrogen atmosphere, producing aluminum nitride while eliminating contaminants. Each method offers distinct advantages in purity, yield, and cost-efficiency, catering to diverse industrial applications such as electronics and ceramics.</p></p>
