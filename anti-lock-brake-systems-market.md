<p><ul><li>One-channel ABS</li><li>Two-channel ABS</li><li>Three-channel ABS</li><li>Four-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-lock Brake Systems (ABS) market features several types based on channel configurations. One-channel ABS is primarily used in single-axle vehicles, enhancing braking control on that axle. Two-channel ABS operates on front and rear axles independently, improving stability. Three-channel ABS, typically found in some SUVs, offers more advanced control with an individual channel for each front wheel and a shared channel for the rear. Four-channel ABS provides the highest level of control by managing each wheel independently, ideal for modern vehicles prioritizing safety.</p></p>
