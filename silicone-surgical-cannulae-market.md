<p><p>The Silicone Surgical Cannulae market is categorized into two primary types: curved cannulae and straight cannulae. Curved cannulae are designed to navigate anatomical structures more easily, making them ideal for procedures requiring precise access, such as liposuction or fat grafting. In contrast, straight cannulae offer straightforward access for injecting or aspirating fluids, commonly used in a variety of surgical applications. Each type serves specific clinical needs, driving demand in surgical settings and enhancing procedural efficiency.</p></p>
