<p><p>The dental intraoral X-rays market encompasses various types of imaging techniques used to diagnose dental conditions. Bitewing X-rays capture images of the upper and lower teeth in one film, helping identify interproximal decay and check the health of supporting bone. Periapical X-rays focus on a particular tooth, revealing its root structure and surrounding bone, crucial for identifying issues below the gum line. Other types include occlusal and panoramic X-rays, which provide broader views for comprehensive diagnostics.</p></p>
