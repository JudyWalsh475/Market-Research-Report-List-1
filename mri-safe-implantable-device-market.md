<p><p>The MRI safe implantable device market is segmented into four categories based on magnetic field strength. Low-to-Mid Field MRI (0.2 to 1.0 Tesla) is often used for specific, less detailed scans. Very-High-Field MRI (3.0 to 7.0 Tesla) provides enhanced resolution for advanced diagnostics, while Ultra-High-Field MRI (above 7.0 Tesla) is utilized for cutting-edge research and specialized medical imaging, focusing on intricate details.</p></p>
