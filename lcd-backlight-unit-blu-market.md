<p><ul><li>Edge Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) market is divided into two main types: edge type and direct type. Edge type backlighting features LEDs positioned along the panel's edges, allowing for a slim and lightweight design, commonly used in portable devices. In contrast, direct type backlighting places LEDs directly behind the LCD panel, offering more uniform brightness and better color accuracy, making it ideal for larger displays like televisions. Each type serves distinct applications based on design and performance requirements.</p></p>
