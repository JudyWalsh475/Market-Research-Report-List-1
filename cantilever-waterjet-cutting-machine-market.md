<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cantilever Waterjet Cutting Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Pressurized Water and Abrasive Mixture. Pressurized Water systems utilize high-pressure water alone to cut materials, ideal for softer substrates like foam or paper. In contrast, Abrasive Mixture systems incorporate abrasive materials into the high-pressure water stream, enabling them to cut through tougher materials like metal or stone. Both types offer precision cutting capabilities, making them suitable for various industrial applications, from manufacturing to artistic endeavors.</p></p>
