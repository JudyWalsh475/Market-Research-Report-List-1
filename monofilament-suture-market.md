<p><strong>The Monofilament Suture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Monofilament Suture</li><li>Non-absorbable Monofilament Suture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monofilament suture market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable monofilament sutures are designed to break down in the body over time, making them ideal for internal wound closures where follow-up removal isn't feasible. Non-absorbable monofilament sutures, on the other hand, are permanent and used for long-term support in areas requiring sustained strength, such as skin sutures and certain surgical applications. Each type plays a crucial role in various surgical procedures and wound management.</p></p>
