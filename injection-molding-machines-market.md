<p><p>The Injection Molding Machines market comprises various types, primarily Mechanical and Hydraulic machines. Mechanical Injection Molding Machines utilize direct mechanical drive systems for precision and speed, offering energy efficiency and lower operational costs. In contrast, Hydraulic Injection Molding Machines rely on hydraulic fluid to generate pressure, providing high force and suitability for larger molds. Both types cater to different production needs, with mechanical machines ideal for smaller, intricate designs, while hydraulic machines excel in heavy-duty applications and larger production runs.</p></p>
