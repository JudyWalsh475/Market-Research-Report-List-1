<p><p>The biohazard waste bags market includes various types of materials, notably HDPE, LDPE, and LLDPE. HDPE bags are known for their strength and resistance to punctures and chemicals, making them ideal for sharp objects and liquid waste. LDPE bags are more flexible and offer good stretchability, suitable for general biohazard waste. LLDPE combines the properties of both HDPE and LDPE, providing enhanced strength and flexibility. Other materials may include biodegradable options or specialized composite bags to meet diverse disposal needs.</p></p>
