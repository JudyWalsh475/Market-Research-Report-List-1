<p><strong>The Smart Medical Loupes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TTL Loupes (through the lens loupes)</li><li>Flip-up Loupes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Medical Loupes market is primarily divided into two types: TTL (Through-the-Lens) loupes and Flip-up loupes. TTL loupes are integrated directly into the lens of the eyeglasses, offering a compact and lightweight design that provides precise magnification and enhanced visual clarity without additional headgear. In contrast, Flip-up loupes feature movable lenses mounted on a frame, allowing users to easily switch between magnified and unobstructed views. Both types improve surgical precision and ergonomics for medical professionals.</p></p>
