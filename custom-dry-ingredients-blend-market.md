<p><p>The custom dry ingredients blend market is categorized into four primary types: flake, flour, bran, and whole form. Flake blends often feature larger, flattened pieces for texture, while flour provides a fine powder ideal for baking and cooking applications. Bran consists of the outer layer of grains, rich in fiber and nutrients, commonly used in health-focused products. Whole form blends maintain the integrity of the entire ingredient, offering versatility and nutritional benefits in various culinary uses.</p></p>
