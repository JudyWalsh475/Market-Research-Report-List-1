<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CdZnTe Radiation Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pixelated</li><li>Coplanar Grid</li><li>Planar-Type</li><li>Quasi-Hemispherical</li><li>Housed Quasi-Hemispherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CdZnTe radiation detectors are categorized into several types based on their design and application. Pixelated detectors use an array of small pixels for spatial resolution; coplanar grid detectors feature electrodes arranged in a grid for improved energy resolution. Planar-type detectors have a simple flat configuration, while quasi-hemispherical detectors offer enhanced detection efficiency through their curved shape. Housed quasi-hemispherical detectors are enclosed, providing protection and stability, making them suitable for various environments and applications in radiation detection.</p></p>
