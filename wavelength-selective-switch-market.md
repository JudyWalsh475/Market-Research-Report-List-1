<p><p>The wavelength selective switch (WSS) market comprises several device types, including LCOS (liquid crystal on silicon) based devices, MEMS (micro-electromechanical systems) based devices, and others. LCOS devices utilize liquid crystals to modulate light efficiently, enabling precise wavelength routing. MEMS devices rely on micro-scale mechanical components for switching, providing flexibility and compactness. Other technologies may include optical waveguides and tunable filters. These variations cater to diverse applications in telecommunications, data centers, and optical networks, enhancing performance and scalability.</p></p>
