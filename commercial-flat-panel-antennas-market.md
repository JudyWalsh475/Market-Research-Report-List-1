<p><strong>The Commercial Flat Panel Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronically Steered</li><li>Mechanically Steered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Flat Panel Antennas market is divided into Electronically Steered and Mechanically Steered types. Electronically Steered Antennas utilize phased array technology, allowing for rapid beam steering and improved tracking of moving targets without physical movement. This enhances performance in dynamic environments. In contrast, Mechanically Steered Antennas rely on physical movement to orient the antenna, offering simplicity and reliability but with slower response times. Both types cater to diverse applications such as telecommunications, satellite communication, and internet connectivity.</p></p>
