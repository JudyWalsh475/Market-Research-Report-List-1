<p><strong>The Ultraviolet (UV) Stabilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizer (HALS)</li><li>UV Absorbers</li><li>Quencher</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Stabilizers market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS)** inhibit photodegradation, extending the lifespan of polymers in outdoor applications. </p><p>2. **UV Absorbers** absorb harmful UV radiation, preventing it from degrading materials. </p><p>3. **Quenchers** neutralize excited states of molecules that can lead to polymer breakdown. </p><p>4. **Other stabilizers** include various additives that enhance UV protection across different materials. Together, these products ensure durability and maintain aesthetic qualities in UV-exposed applications.</p></p>
