<p><p>The semiconductor thermal evaporators market is primarily categorized into single effect and multi effect evaporation types. Single effect evaporation involves a straightforward process where a single stage of heat is applied to evaporate materials, suitable for small-scale applications. In contrast, multi effect evaporation utilizes multiple stages to enhance efficiency and reduce energy consumption, making it ideal for larger scale operations. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, influencing production efficiency and material quality in the industry.</p></p>
