<p><ul><li>Horizontal Dryer</li><li>Vertical Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary grain dryers are essential for moisture control in grain storage, categorized primarily into horizontal and vertical dryers. Horizontal dryers feature a long, rectangular design allowing for a continuous flow of grain and efficient heat distribution, making them suitable for large volumes. Vertical dryers, on the other hand, utilize a compact, upright structure that offers efficient airflow and space-saving benefits but may have a lower throughput. Both types aim to enhance grain quality and extend shelf life through effective drying processes.</p></p>
