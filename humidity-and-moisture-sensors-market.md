<p><strong>The Humidity and Moisture Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humidity and moisture sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: relative humidity sensors and absolute humidity sensors. Relative humidity sensors measure the ratio of the current moisture level in the air to the maximum moisture level possible at a given temperature, providing a percentage reading. In contrast, absolute humidity sensors quantify the actual amount of water vapor present in the air, expressed in grams per cubic meter. Both types play critical roles in various applications, including HVAC systems, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes.</p></p>
