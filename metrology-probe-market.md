<p><p>The metrology probe market includes various types of precision measurement tools, primarily categorized into machine tool probes and CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) probes. Machine tool probes are integrated into machining equipment to ensure proper alignment and dimensional accuracy during the manufacturing process. In contrast, CMM probes are used in sophisticated measuring systems to assess the physical geometrical characteristics of an object, allowing for high-precision inspection and quality control. Both types are essential for enhancing manufacturing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
