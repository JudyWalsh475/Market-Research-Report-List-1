<p><p>The semiconductor dielectric etching equipment market comprises two main types: wet etching equipment and dry etching equipment. Wet etching utilizes liquid chemicals to remove material, offering advantages like high selectivity and uniformity, making it suitable for bulk processes. Dry etching, on the other hand, employs gases or plasmas to etch materials, providing precise control over etching profiles and enabling the fabrication of intricate structures. Both types play critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing to achieve desired surface qualities and dimensions.</p></p>
