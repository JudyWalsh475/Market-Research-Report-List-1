<p><p>The industrial indoor overhead cranes market is primarily categorized into two types: double girder cranes and single girder cranes. Double girder cranes feature two bridge beams, providing enhanced stability and load capacity, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Conversely, single girder cranes utilize a single beam, allowing for a lighter structure and lower operational costs, suitable for lighter loads and smaller indoor spaces. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing their market demand and application areas.</p></p>
