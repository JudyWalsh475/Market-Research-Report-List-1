<p><p>Electron microscopy is a vital tool in industrial applications, utilized for its high-resolution imaging capabilities. The two primary types are Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM) and Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM). TEM offers detailed internal structure analysis at the atomic level, suitable for materials science and nanotechnology. SEM, on the other hand, provides surface topography and composition information, making it ideal for quality control and failure analysis. Other forms include Scanning Transmission Electron Microscopy (STEM) and electron tomography, expanding analytical capabilities across various industries.</p></p>
