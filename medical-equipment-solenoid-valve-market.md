<p><strong>The Medical Equipment Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Acting</li><li>Pilot Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical equipment solenoid valve market can be divided into two main types: direct acting and pilot operated. Direct acting solenoid valves operate directly on the fluid flow, requiring less power and ideal for low-pressure applications. In contrast, pilot operated solenoid valves utilize a small pilot valve to control a larger flow of liquid or gas, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Both types are essential for precise control in medical devices, ensuring reliability and efficiency in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
