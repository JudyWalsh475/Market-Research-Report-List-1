<p><ul><li>Directional Doppler</li><li>Non Directional Doppler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trolley-based Doppler ultrasound systems are categorized into directional and non-directional Doppler markets. Directional Doppler systems measure the velocity and flow direction of blood or tissue movement, providing detailed hemodynamic information essential for diagnosing cardiovascular conditions. In contrast, non-directional Doppler systems assess flow velocity without determining the direction, offering a simpler analysis suitable for various applications. Both types enhance medical diagnostics, with directional systems favored for complex assessments and non-directional systems utilized for routine examinations.</p></p>
