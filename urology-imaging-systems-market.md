Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides detailed imaging of the urinary system using magnetic fields, effective for complex diagnoses. Computed Tomography (CT) offers cross-sectional imaging for a comprehensive view of anatomical structures and abnormalities. Each modality serves distinct diagnostic purposes in urology.</p></p>
