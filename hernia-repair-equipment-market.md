<p><p>The hernia repair equipment market primarily consists of two segments: hernia mesh and mesh fixators. Hernia mesh is a biocompatible material used to reinforce the abdominal wall during hernia repair procedures, enhancing healing and reducing recurrence. Mesh fixators, on the other hand, are devices or tools that secure the mesh in place, ensuring stability during recovery. Both components are critical in modern surgical techniques, improving outcomes and patient safety in hernia management.</p></p>
