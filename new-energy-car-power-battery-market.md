<p><p>The New Energy Car Power Battery market is primarily categorized into three types: Ternary batteries, Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) batteries, and other specialized batteries. LFP batteries, known for their thermal stability and safety, provide longevity and are favored for cost-effectiveness. Other battery types include solid-state and sodium-ion batteries, which are emerging as alternatives with unique benefits.</p></p>
