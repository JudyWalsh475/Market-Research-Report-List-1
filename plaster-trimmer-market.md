<p><p>The plaster trimmer market is divided into two main types: wet plaster trimmers and dry plaster trimmers. Wet plaster trimmers are designed for use with freshly applied plaster, featuring tools that efficiently shape and smooth the surface before it sets. In contrast, dry plaster trimmers are utilized on hardened plaster, allowing for precise finishing and detailing work. Both types cater to different stages of the plastering process, serving various needs in construction and renovation projects.</p></p>
