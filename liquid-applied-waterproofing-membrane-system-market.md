<p><p>Liquid Applied Waterproofing Membrane Systems are crucial for protecting structures from water infiltration. The NCM (Non-Carbonated Membrane) type features a solid, durable layer that effectively blocks moisture, often used in areas requiring high resistance to chemicals and UV exposure. Conversely, the NCA (Carbonated Membrane) type involves a more flexible membrane system that accommodates structural movements and temperature changes, making it suitable for diverse applications. Both types cater to different environmental conditions, offering tailored solutions for waterproofing needs.</p></p>
