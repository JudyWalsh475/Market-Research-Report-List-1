<p><p>The shaftless encoder market consists of three primary types: magnetic, capacitive, and optical encoders. </p><p>Magnetic encoders utilize magnetic fields to determine position and movement, offering robustness in harsh environments. Capacitive encoders rely on changes in capacitance to provide precise measurements, ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. Optical encoders use light sources and sensors to detect position, providing high resolution and accuracy. Each type serves distinct applications in automation, robotics, and industrial machinery, catering to specific performance and environmental needs.</p></p>
