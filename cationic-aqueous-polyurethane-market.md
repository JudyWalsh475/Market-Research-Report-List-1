<p><p>The cationic aqueous polyurethane market is primarily segmented into two types: carboxylic acid type polyurethane and sulfonic acid type polyurethane. Carboxylic acid type polyurethane features carboxylic groups, enhancing its affinity for substrates and adhesion properties, making it suitable for coatings and adhesives. Sulfonic acid type polyurethane, characterized by sulfonic groups, offers improved thermal stability and water solubility, appealing for applications like textile and leather finishing. Both types are eco-friendly alternatives in various industries due to their water-based formulations.</p></p>
