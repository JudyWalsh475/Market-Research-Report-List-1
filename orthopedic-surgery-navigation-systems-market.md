<p><p>Orthopedic surgery navigation systems enhance precision in surgical procedures by providing real-time guidance. Optical surgical navigation systems utilize cameras and markers to track instruments and anatomical structures, ensuring accurate positioning during surgeries. Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems employ electromagnetic fields to detect the position of instruments, allowing for flexible movement and positioning. Other navigation systems may include hybrid technologies or advanced imaging tools that integrate various methodologies for improved accuracy and user experience, catering to diverse surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
