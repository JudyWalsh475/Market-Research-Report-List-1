<p><p>The Polyethylene Glycol (PEG) market is generally segmented into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I PEG encompasses low molecular weight PEGs, typically used in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and personal care products for their solubilizing and humidity-retaining properties. Type II PEG comprises higher molecular weight variants, commonly utilized in industrial applications, such as lubricants and coatings. Each type serves distinct functions across various industries, catering to specific requirements for performance, safety, and formulation.</p></p>
