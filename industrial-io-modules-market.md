<p><p>Industrial I/O modules are crucial for data acquisition and control in automation systems. PLC I/O modules connect programmable logic controllers to field devices, allowing real-time processing for manufacturing tasks. DCS I/O modules interface with distributed control systems, enabling centralized control and monitoring of complex processes across multiple locations. PC-based I/O modules connect directly to computer systems, offering flexibility and advanced data processing capabilities for industrial applications, enhancing integration and efficiency in modern manufacturing environments.</p></p>
